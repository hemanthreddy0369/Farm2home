Farm2Home is an innovative platform connecting local farmers directly with consumers.

It helps farmers sell their crops at fair prices without middlemen.

Consumers can find fresh, locally grown produce easily.

The platform promotes sustainable and eco-friendly agriculture.

Farm2Home provides real-time crop availability and pricing details.

Users can search for crops and find farmers near their location.

It encourages small-scale farmers to increase their income.

The app integrates 3D visuals and interactive UI for a modern experience.

It is part of the Viksit Bharat Buildathon 2025 initiative.

Farm2Home empowers both farmers and consumers, building a stronger agricultural ecosystem.
